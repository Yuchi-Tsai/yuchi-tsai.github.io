Welcome / Bienvenue

Hi, I’m Yu-Chi, but feel free to call me Cassie. Born and raised in Taiwan, I took a leap to study in France, where I’m currently completing my Master’s degree in International Business at MBS, set to graduate this July. Before that, my background was in healthcare and the medical industry, which led me to work in medical market research. My journey has taken me to some wonderful places and allowed me to explore new fields.

Living in France has been an eye-opening experience. The country’s rich diversity and culture have given me countless opportunities to collaborate with people from all over the world, broadening my perspective in ways I never imagined. After completing my courses, I moved to Paris to finish my thesis while working remotely for an investment securities company, where I gained valuable insights into the business world.

My journey has been anything but linear—I’ve always been eager to step outside my comfort zone and explore new opportunities.

Now, I’m in Munich, where I’m interning at Huawei’s Antenna Lab, working in the field of digital marketing for B2B business. It’s a great opportunity to expand my skill set and learn from industry experts, and I’m excited to see where this journey takes me.

On this site, you’ll find detailed information about my work experience, both past and present, as well as my projects. You can also find the link to my LinkedIn profile at the bottom.

I love exchanging ideas, learning from different perspectives, and embracing new challenges. If my journey resonates with you, feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect and hear from you!
