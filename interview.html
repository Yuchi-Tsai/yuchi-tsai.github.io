B2B Strategic Interviews with Co-Founder of YEESMA

Challenges in e-motor industry: Balancing Performance, Cost, and Sustainability

Rare-earth magnets are costly, environmentally harmful, and predominantly sourced from a single country, creating significant supply chain risks. The co-founder of YEESMA explained how their EESM-based axial flux technology eliminates the need for rare-earth magnets, achieving a 50% reduction in BOM costs and 70% lower CO₂ emissions.

Interview of Technical Insights into YEESMA Motor Technology

Rare‒Earth Free vs. Bulkier Motor & Expensive Inverter

To reduce the use of rare-earth magnets and achieve the same torque and power ratings, but the overall package becomes 30–40% larger and the inverter requires higher phase current. In the end, it is not cheaper for the whole packaging.
As an axial flux EESM, YEESMA is more compact, requires lower phase current. It brings not onlt affordable motor but also a cheaper inverter.


B2B Strategic Interview with the Technical Director of Aros

Performance, Sustainability, and Application-Specific Design

With over 35 years at Aros, Magnus Wide, Technical Director at Aros, shares how decades of expertise in power electronics and drive systems are translated into customized solutions for demanding industrial and automotive applications. Since its establishment in 1970, Aros has continuously transformed accumulated know-how into a scalable solution platform, now enriched by its integration into the global Vandewiele Group, enabling worldwide manufacturing support and complementary R&D services.


Ecodrive: Interview with Aros' Lead of Motor Development

A Fully Integrated Electric Drive Platform for Sustainable Commercial Vehicles

Building on decades of experience with permanent magnet motors, Aros has expanded its portfolio with rare-earth-free and EESM-based technologies, delivering a fully integrated system where the motor, power electronics, and control software are designed together. This solution offers greater flexibility around cost, supply chain resilience, and sustainability targets.


Aros Electric Motor Development Lab Tour

1/3: Aros' Tehnical Lab Tour

This is Aros’ electric motor development lab, where non–rare-earth motor concepts are tested and characterized. The lab supports full in-house validation, including environmental, vibration, and EMC testing, and current work focuses on the Ecodrive sensorless motor platform.
